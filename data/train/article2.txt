According to the ANI report, an FIR was filed against late Sushant Singh Rajput's girlfriend Rhea Chakraborty at his hometown, Patna. The tweet read as''FIR registered against actor Rhea Chakraborty under various sections, including abetment of suicide, on the complaint of #SushantSinghRajput's father: Sanjay Singh, Inspector General, Patna Central Zone''. They further added, ''A four-member team has been sent to Mumbai. The team will collect case diary and other important documents from Mumbai Police: Sanjay Singh, Inspector General, Patna Central Zone''.
